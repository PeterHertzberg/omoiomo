WHAT'S OMOiOMO?

Hello! I'm Peter and OMOiOMO is my vessel to try to find my way back to that magical land of stories where I would dream away my days as a child...

The driving value in OMOiOMO is to serve the audience, always striving to create stories that have a strong emotional impact. I believe everything in the story, and the creating of it, that doesn't support this is a waste and try to cut them away.

The underlying principle that I try to follow in my process of creating is that of "Shibumi" or "sophisticated simplicity".

I believe what you achieve is not the sum of what you should do tomorrow, or what you should have done yesterday, but the sum of your nows, of how you use your moments. It's the process and not the goal that drives you forward. By amplifying the learning through cycles of kata => mushin => hansei => kaizen, or routine, without mind -or- flow, reflection, small improvements, I constantly look to simplify the process by removing frictions and wastes.

"Learning from yesterday to today make tomorrows path forward easier"
